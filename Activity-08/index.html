Full Body Beginner Workout

Workout Steps

  1. Warm-up (5 minutes walk or jog in place)
  2. Push-ups - 3 sets of 10 reps
  3. Bodyweight squats - 3 sets of 15 reps
  4. Plank - hold for 1 minute
  5. Cool down and stretch - 5 minutes

Equipment Needed

  • Yoga mat
  • Water bottle
  • Resistance bands
  • Dumbbells

Nutrition & Sample Diet Plan

A balanced diet is crucial to support your fitness goals. Here's a simple daily meal plan to fuel your workouts and recovery:

  • Breakfast: Oatmeal with bananas and almond butter, boiled eggs, black coffee or green tea
  • Snack: Greek yogurt with mixed berries
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken breast, brown rice, and steamed vegetables
  • Snack: Handful of almonds and an apple
  • Dinner: Baked salmon, sweet potato, and spinach salad
  • Hydration: Drink at least 2 liters of water daily
