History of Vizag

Visakhapatnam, popularly called Vizag, is an ancient port city on the east coast of India. It has a rich history dating back to the Mauryan era and was an important trade center for many dynasties including the Kalinga and the British Empire.

Natural Beauty

Nestled between the Eastern Ghats and the Bay of Bengal, Vizag boasts stunning beaches like Ramakrishna Beach and Rishikonda Beach. The city is also home to lush hills, serene waterfalls, and the famous Araku Valley nearby.

Cultural Heritage

Vizag is a melting pot of traditions with festivals like Sankranti and Diwali celebrated with great fervor. The city is known for its classical dance forms, vibrant handicrafts, and delicious Andhra cuisine.

Plan Your Visit

Whether you want to relax on the beach, explore ancient temples, or taste authentic South Indian food, Vizag welcomes you with open arms. The best time to visit is between October and March when the weather is pleasant.
